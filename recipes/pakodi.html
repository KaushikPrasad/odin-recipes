Pakodi

Description

Pakodi is a popular snack that is enjoyed all over India and is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a quick snack, an appetizer or even as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • Gram flour (also known as besan)
  • Salt
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Finely chopped onions
  • Green chillies
  • Coriander leaves
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, add gram flour, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Add water gradually and mix the batter well to get a smooth and thick consistency. The batter should not be too thin or too thick.
  3. Add the vegetables or other ingredients of your choice to the batter and mix well so that they are coated evenly with the batter.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, drop small portions of the batter-coated vegetables or other ingredients into the oil.
  5. Fry the pakodis in the hot oil until they are golden brown and crispy. Stir occasionally to ensure even frying.
  6. Once the pakodis are done, remove them from the oil using a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel to absorb any excess oil.
  7. Serve the pakodis hot with your favorite chutney or ketchup.
